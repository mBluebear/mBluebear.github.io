About Me

My name is Quynh Nguyen, I am a graphic design and multimedia specialist based in Ottawa, Canada. I love being creative and want to take challenges. To me, being a graphic designer, not just make the world pretty but to help people solve design-related problems in a smart and unique way. My strengths are designing  product labels, logos, packages and illustrating.

Please refer to the portfolio section for examples of my work and don’t hesitate to give me a shout if you have any questions or just want to say hello.
